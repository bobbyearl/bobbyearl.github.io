Welcome to Geocoinfest U.S. 2011! We are honored to have been selected to host the 5th Geocoinfest event here in the Carolinas.

This event will bring together geocoin enthusiast, vendors, designers, collectors and geocachers from all over the world in one location for buying, trading, and fellowship. You may even learn a thing or two about geocoins and geocaching.

There will be several events during the weekend of October 14th to the 16th, 2011, with the main event, Geocoinfest, being held on Saturday, October 15th, 2011. The event will be held at the Baxter Hood Center in Rock Hill, SC. We are just south of Charlotte, NC.

Keep checking back for more information. See you in October 2011!
